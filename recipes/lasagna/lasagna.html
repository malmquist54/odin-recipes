Lasagna

Description

This lasagna is a family favorite for the Almquist's. It features several kinds of cheese, two kinds of ground meat, and a sauce that highlights a couple sources of heat.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 lb hot italian sausage
  • 3-16oz cans/jars of red sauce

Steps

  1. Brown both kinds of meat
  2. Add sause to browned meat
  3. layer with cheeses and pasta
